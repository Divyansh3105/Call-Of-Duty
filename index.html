What is Call Of Duty?

Call of Duty is a legendary first-person shooter video game franchise created by Infinity Ward and published by Activision. Debuting in 2003, the series began with World War II settings before evolving into modern, futuristic, and even battle royale warfare. Known for its intense single-player campaigns, cinematic storytelling, and competitive multiplayer modes, Call of Duty has become a global gaming phenomenon. Iconic titles like Modern Warfare, Black Ops, and Warzone have shaped the FPS genre and pop culture. With cutting-edge graphics, immersive gameplay, and massive fan support, Call of Duty continues to redefine action gaming across generations. Semper Fi!
