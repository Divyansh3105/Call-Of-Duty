No Campaign. All Combat.

Multiplayer-Focused Warfare

Call of Duty: Black Ops 4 (2018) broke tradition by removing the campaign and focusing on refined multiplayer, an expanded Zombies mode, and the franchise’s first battle royale — Blackout. Specialists returned, each with unique gear and tactical abilities, adding strategy and identity to the chaos.

Modes of Mayhem

Competitive Multiplayer

Specialists and tactical play emphasize team coordination and unique roles.

Zombies Chaos

Multiple storylines, mythologies, and new mechanics in epic undead survival maps.

Blackout Mode

The franchise’s first foray into battle royale. Iconic maps, weapons, and fast-paced tactics.

Iconic Specialists

BO4 featured 10+ specialists with distinct abilities. From Seraph’s Annihilator to Torque’s barricades, they brought flavor and variety to each battle. Mastering their synergy with team tactics was key to victory.
