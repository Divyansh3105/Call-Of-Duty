CALL OF DUTY: Advanced Warfare

Power Changes Everything

A Futuristic Fight

In Call of Duty: Advanced Warfare (2014), join Jack Mitchell and the powerful Atlas Corporation in a world where military power is driven by advanced exoskeletons and private armies. With an intense story and Hollywood star power from Kevin Spacey, this game redefined future warfare with jetpacks, high-tech gadgets, and tactical freedom.
