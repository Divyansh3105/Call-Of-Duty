The Beginning of a Legacy

Step into the game that changed everything. In Call of Duty 4: Modern Warfare (2007), join Captain Price and the legendary SAS as they confront a global threat across war-torn cities and volatile regions. With its gripping narrative, unforgettable missions like “All Ghillied Up,” and a bold new take on modern combat, this campaign set the standard for storytelling in first-person shooters.

Iconic Arsenal

From the M4A1 to the AK-47, Call of Duty 4 introduced a wide arsenal of modern weaponry. Each gun felt distinct, deadly, and satisfying. With customizable loadouts and killstreak rewards like UAVs and airstrikes, it set the template for multiplayer shooters to come.
